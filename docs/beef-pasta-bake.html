Cheesy Beef Pasta Bake

with tomato sauce and spinach

Ingredients

  • 227g Ground Beef
  • 170g Penne
  • 56g Baby Spinach
  • 5g Italian Herb Spice Blend
  • 1/2 cup Marinara Sauce
  • 3/4 cup Mozarella Cheese, shredded
  • 10g Cream Sauce Spice Belnd
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

Cook Penne:

  • Preheat broiler to high. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  • Wash and dry produce
  • To boiling water, add penne
  • Cook for 10 - 12 min, until tender but still firm to the bite
  • Reserve 1 cup pasta water
  • Strain penne, then return to the same pot, off heat

Prep Veggies:

  • Meanwhile, peel, then cut onion into 1/2-inch pieces
  • Roughly chop spinach

Cook Beef and Onions:

  • In a large non-stick pan, heat 1 tsp oil over medium-high
  • When pan is hot, add onions and beef
  • Cook for 3-5 min, breaking up beef into smaller pieces, until no pink remains
  • Drain and discard excess fat
  • Season with salt and pepper

Make Tomato Sauce:

  • To pan with beef, add Italian Herb Spice Blend and Cream Sauce Spice Blend
  • Cook for 30 sec, stirring often, until fragrant
  • Add marinara sauce and reserved pasta water
  • Bring to boil over high
  • Once boiling, reduce heat to medium and cook for 3-5 min, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened
  • Season with pepper

Assemble and Broil Pasta:

  • Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish
  • To pot with penne, add sauce, spinach, half the cheese, and 1 tbsp butter. Stir until spinach wilts
  • Transfer pasta to the prepared baking dish
  • Sprinkle remaing cheese over top
  • Broil in the middle of oven for 1-3 min, until cheese is melted and golden

Plate and Serve:

  • Divide pasta bake between plates
