Applewood Pork Pizzas

with BBQ sauce

Ingredients

  • 250g Ground Pork
  • 2 Flatbread
  • 4 tbsp Tomato Sauce Base
  • 3/4 cup Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
  • 7g Applewood Smoke Spice
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Sweet Bell Pepper
  • 4 tbsp BBQ Sauce
  • 1 Green Onion
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

Prep Veggies:

  • Preheat oven to 450Â°F
  • Wash and dry produce
  • Peel, then cut onion into 1/4-inch slices
  • Cut pepper into 1/4-inch slices
  • Thinly slice green onion

Bake Flatbreads:

  • Line baking sheet with aluminum foil and arrange flatbreads
  • Brush with 1/2 tbsp oil
  • Toast flatbreads in the middle of oven for 2-3 min, until softened

Cook Pork:

  • Meanwhile, heat a large non-stick pan over medium-high
  • When pan is hot, add 1 tbsp oil, then pork, onions and peppers
  • Cook for 4-6 min, breaking up pork into smaller pieces, until veggies soften and no pink remains
  • Add Applewood Smoke Spice and season with salt and pepper
  • Cook for 30 sec, stirring often, until fragrant

Assemble and Bake Pizzas:

  • Spread tomato sauce base over flatbreads. Top with mozarella, veggies, and pork
  • Bake in the middle of oven for 7-8 min, untl browned and crisp

Plate and Serve:

  • Cut pizzas into slices and divide between plates
  • Sprinkle green onions and drizzle BBQ sauce over top
